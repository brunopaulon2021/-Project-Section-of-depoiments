The importance of a testimonial.

Testimonials can be a great marketing tool for any business. Whether you were approached to write one, or decided on your own, your testimony needs to be as detailed and persuasive as possible. To make a strong statement, start by describing the problem, then explain how the contracted product or service resolved the issue. End with a recommendation.

Should my testimonial be extensive?

The amount and type of information to include about you will depend on the product or service provided. The testimony needs to be relatively short, so it's important to leave out irrelevant information.
